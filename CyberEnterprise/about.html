Our Story

From a small research lab to the world's leading cybernetic enhancement provider

Founded in 2035 by Dr. Elena Nakamura and a team of visionary bioengineers, CyberCorp began with a single mission: to transcend the limitations of the human body through seamless integration of cutting-edge technology and organic systems.


What started as a breakthrough neural interface developed for medical applications quickly evolved into the first generation of true cybernetic enhancements. Our proprietary Synth-Neural™ technology changed the landscape of human augmentation forever, providing unprecedented levels of integration between artificial components and the human nervous system.


Today, CyberCorp stands at the forefront of the enhancement revolution, with research facilities spanning four continents and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible. Our team of over 3,000 scientists, engineers, and medical professionals continues to redefine human potential, one enhancement at a time.


We believe in a future where physical limitations are optional, where human ingenuity and technological advancement merge to create something greater than the sum of its parts. At CyberCorp, we're not just building better body parts—we're building a better future.
