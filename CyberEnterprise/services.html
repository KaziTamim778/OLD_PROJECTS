Frequently Asked Questions

What voids my warranty?

Your warranty becomes void if unauthorized technicians perform modifications, if you use non-approved components, or if damage occurs from improper use such as exposure to incompatible electromagnetic fields or extreme environments. Self-repair attempts will also void warranty protection.

How do I file a warranty claim?

Contact our service center through your neural interface's emergency protocol, visit any certified clinic location, or use our secure comms channel. Our technicians will perform a remote diagnostic, and if necessary, schedule an immediate service appointment. All warranty claims require verification of your enhancement's registration code.

Can I upgrade my warranty after purchase?

Yes, warranty upgrades are available within the first 90 days after installation. After this period, we require a full diagnostic exam (priced at 500 CR) to verify system condition before allowing warranty upgrades. Lifetime warranty upgrades are only available for enhancements less than 18 months old.

Are software malfunctions covered?

All firmware and system software issues are covered under every warranty tier. However, third-party applications and neural plugins are not covered unless they were installed by our certified technicians and are on our approved compatibility list. We provide quarterly security and stability updates regardless of warranty status.
